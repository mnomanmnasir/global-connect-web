Habib Bank Limited

Security & Surveillance Infrastructure

Global Connect Synergy (Pvt) Ltd implemented a comprehensive security and surveillance solution for HBL, covering CCTV, access control, screening systems, and centralized monitoring infrastructure. The project was designed to strengthen physical security, optimize access management, and ensure seamless system performance.

Key Highlights

  • Installed, tested, and commissioned CCTV systems for complete surveillance coverage.
  • Deployed access control, thermal screening cameras, and speed gates for secure and safe entry management.
  • Provided troubleshooting for road blockers and barriers to maintain smooth operations.
  • Planned deployment of PA systems, UVSS, barrier controls, and walkthrough gates for enhanced security.
  • Commissioned and optimized the central control room for real time monitoring and issue resolution.
